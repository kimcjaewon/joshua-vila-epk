About the artist

Joshua Vila is a Filipino R&B singer-songwriter out of Los Angeles, writing soulful, late-night records about love, distance and everything in between.

Equal parts smooth and confessional, Vila's sound sits in the modern alt-R&B pocket — warm vocals, intimate production and lyrics that read like text messages you meant to send. Since 2024 he's released a steady run of singles, from the breakout "REVEALING" to the latest "GRAVITY," together pulling in 180K+ streams.

On stage he's just as comfortable in an intimate room as on a festival platform, having performed across LA's R&B and Filipino-American scenes — including upcoming nights with the ise collective.
